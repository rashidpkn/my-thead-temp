Rejuvenate - Product Information

Reduce, reuse and recycle with Rejuvenate!

Rejuvenate is made from 100% recycled plastic. In fact, for every metre of Rejuvenate curtain fabric produced, 81 plastic bottles have been diverted from landfill to be used.

Despite its recycled origins, Rejuvenate fabric is as soft and beautifully textured as any of our polyester sheers, and is available in cool and warmer tones that are sure to suit many homes.

 

Specifications

  • Width

    • Minimum: 750mm
    • Maximum: 5000mm (single), 7500mm (centre opening)

  • Drop

    • Minimum: 500mm
    • Maximum: 3060mm

  • Material

    • 100% recycled plastic polyester
    • Linen-look fabric
    • 5mm weighted hem

  • Tracking

    • Designer tracks over 2300mm wide require simple assembly
    • Designer track height 15mm
    • Curtains underslung on Designer track

  • Brackets

    • Standard bracket depth 70mm
    • Extension bracket depth 160mm
