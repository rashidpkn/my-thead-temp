Panel Glide blinds to suit any Aussie home

Light and easy to operate, these blinds consist of sleek fabric panels that glide effortlessly across a simple headrail track. Thanks to this user-friendly, smooth-glide operating system, light control has never been easier. With the simple slide of a wand, you can easily adjust the level of sunlight that enters your rooms.

When fully open, panel blinds neatly stack behind one another, allowing maximum light into the space. When closed, they provide a tasteful fabric screen that will enhance any interior. Whether you’re looking to give your home a freshen up or need to complement your existing decor, we have a range of styles, colours and designs to suit all tastes.

Our blockout panel glide blinds give you the choice of complete privacy or lots of light. Their insulating fabric also helps to regulate the temperature of your rooms and prevent harmful UV rays from getting through. For spaces that require a little more natural light, sunscreen blinds keep out heat and glare without casting rooms into darkness. Meanwhile, light-filtering blinds strike the perfect balance between blockout and sunscreen fabrics, helping to eliminate harsh UV rays while still letting ample light in, keeping your living spaces bright and airy.

The Curtain and Blind Company offers an extensive range of panel glide blinds, delivered Australia-wide. They are the perfect solution for extra-large windows, sliding or patio doors, and can also be used as a room divider. Hugely flexible, the panels can be stacked to the right or the left, or they can be split in the middle and are available in a variety of attractive colours.

Blockout panel glide blinds are ideal for bedrooms. The wide panels reflect daylight to help create a sleep friendly environment. Ready to start your day after a good night’s sleep? Then just pull open the blind and let the sunlight in!
